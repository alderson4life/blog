Mr.Robot

Mr. Robot is a show that skillfully blends technical hacking and psychological thrills to create a truly unique viewing experience. From a technical perspective, the show is meticulous in its attention to detail, accurately depicting the hacking process and showcasing the power of technology in the modern world. However, it's the psychological aspect that really sets the show apart, as it delves deep into the complex emotions and motivations of its characters, leaving viewers on the edge of their seats with each new twist and turn. Whether you're a fan of technology or just enjoy a good psychological thriller, Mr. Robot is a must-watch show that delivers on all fronts.

Movies and Shows That Were Referenced or Homaged in Mr. Robot

Lost (2004-2010)
Whiteroses machine similar to what the others were trying to do. Both are known as McGuffins. In Mr. Robot, it's fake. Same with Lost. Lost ends with an eye opening, Mr. Robot ends with an eye opening.

The Sopranos (1999-2007)
4x4 is influenced by the sopranos episode pine barrens. Also the boardwalk in the series finale of Mr. Robot is somewhat of a homage to 2x13 dream sequence of the Sopranos.

Alf (1986-1990)
The character alf is in 2x6 of Mr. Robot.

Knight Rider (1982-1986)
The theme song of Knight Rider is needle dropped in 3x7 of Mr. Robot.

The X-Files (1993-2018)
The character Invisigoth of X-Files is very similar to Darlene.

Steins Gate (2011-2015)
In whiterose's room, there are numbers which is a homage to this tv show.

Boardwalk Empire (2010-2014)
When Tyrell shoots Elliot in Hello, Elliot seems to be a homage to the tv show.

Vanderpump Rules (2013-)
In the season two premiere, someone watches the reality tv show on their television.

The Bob Newhart Show (1972-1978)
Homaged in 2x6 of Mr. Robot.

Breaking Bad (2008-2013)
In 4x7 of Mr. Robot, Mr. Robot says “I am the gun” a twist on when Walter White of breaking bad says “I am the one who knocks.” Elliot tells Darlene “I wanted this. I liked it.” Is a reference to Walter White's final speech to Skyler in the Breaking Bad series finale.
